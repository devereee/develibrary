About Us

At Develibrary, we're passionate about connecting readers with the stories and knowledge that inspire them. Established with the vision of creating a vibrant community of book lovers, our company offers a diverse selection of books that cater to all tastes, from timeless classics to the latest bestsellers across various genres. Our team is dedicated to curating a rich and varied inventory, ensuring that our customers always have access to captivating stories and insightful information.

Our CEO

Refinaldi Aldrian, is the visionary founder and CEO of Develibrary, a company dedicated to fostering a love of reading and making literature accessible to all. With a lifelong passion for books and an entrepreneurial spirit, he launched Develibrary to create a welcoming space for readers of all ages and preferences.
